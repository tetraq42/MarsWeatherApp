About Mars Weather

This application displays the latest weather data from Mars, collected by NASA's instruments on the Red Planet. The Martian sol is slightly longer than an Earth day at 24 hours and 39 minutes.

Mars has a much thinner atmosphere than Earth, resulting in more extreme temperature variations and lower atmospheric pressure. The average temperature on Mars is about -60°C (-80°F), with temperatures at the equator sometimes reaching 20°C (68°F).
